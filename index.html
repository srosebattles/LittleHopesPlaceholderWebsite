Little Hopes

Because a single thread of hope is a powerful thing


Our Mission

Little Hopes exists for community members to help each other live with dignity by providing support and hope to those who are struggling. We provide our clients with household items, car repairs, medical costs, school supplies, clothing, holiday and birthday gifts, and other items they need but could not otherwise afford. We aim to provide stability and dignity while meeting our clients needs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive environment based in mutual respect where neighbors can give and receive support. Little Hopes believes it is not enough to survive. We must help each other break the cycles of poverty. When we help our neighbors thrive, our community will also thrive.

Where We Work Together

Our clients (who are also Little Hopes supporters themselves) live in St. Anthony or in the Hamline-Midway, Dale-Thomas ("Frogtown"), Como Park, and Union Park neighborhoods in St. Paul. We welcome supporters and donors from all over the Twin Cities and beyond!

Almost all group communication happens in our Facebook community, although we do our best to accomodate clients and supporters who need to communicate in other ways.
